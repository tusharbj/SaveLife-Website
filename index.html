About Organ Donation

Organ donation is a life-saving medical procedure that allows healthy organs and tissues to be transplanted into patients suffering from end-stage organ failure. It is regulated, ethical, and performed under strict medical supervision.

A single donor can save up to eight lives through organ donation and improve over 75 lives through tissue donation. Our platform promotes awareness, transparency, and responsible donor registration.
